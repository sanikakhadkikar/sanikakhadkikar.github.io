Gravitational-wave observations of binary neutron star mergers offer a unique window into the equation of state (EoS) of dense matter. However, the priors we place on tidal deformability parameters can significantly bias our inferences if they don't accurately reflect the underlying physics.

This project investigates how prior choices and waveform model systematics affect EoS constraints, with the aim of developing better-informed priors for the LIGO/Virgo/KAGRA O5 observing run and next-generation detectors.
